BELT CONVEYOR SPARE

Screw Conveyor

Screw conveyors are industrial equipment used in transporting bulk quantities of granular solids (e.g., powder, grains, granules), semi-solids, liquids, and even non-flowing materials from one point to another.

Superstructure

All conveyors are built with frames and supports welded together, making each section a superstructure, and it is the most rugged standard in the industry , its mostly used in industries..

Roller With Bracket

role of fixing roller, a supporting belt, which is an important support structure in the belt conveyor. The roller bracket is made of high-quality materials but also has stable performance, long service life characteristics.

Hopper with Grizly

Grizzly hopper are used to convey coal, lignite or any fuel of particular size in mm to process further into conveyor belt. Single reduction unit drives the main rotating plate picks up the components while moving upwards.

Transoms

The rollers are fixed to the conveyor's frame by means of brackets or transoms. The idlers, created by the assembly of rollers and brackets or transoms, give the shape to the belt..

Bagasse Sliding Gates

Sliding Gates

Slide Gates consist of a rigid frame mounted to the bottom of the storage device or conveyor. A slide plate is located inside the frame that either opens or closes against the flow of material.

External & Internal Scraper
